Canoeing App

Android

Application developed in partnership with Brazilian Canoeing Confederation in order to monitor and improve canoeing athletes performance in trainings and competitions as Olympics Rio 2016. There are two applications: one runs in a smartphone that is placed in the center of the mass of the canoe or kayak and it is responsible for collecting data from device sensors as accelerometer, gyroscope and GPS. The data is processed in real time to generate metrics as speed, distance, paddling frequency and efficacy considering the canoe movement. All these data are sent under a private network established by the smartphone and a tablet used by the coach, where he can analyse and orient the athletes in order to improve their performance.

Android Native Development, Multi-threading, GreenDAO (Offline Storage), Realm, Sync Providers, Crashlytics (Crash Reporting), Bluetooth, Device Sensors, Geolocalization, GreenRobot Event Bus

Ambulance Ocurrence Manager

Android

The objective of the application is improve the communication between the control center and the public ambulances of the state of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The application runs in a tablet placed in the ambulances in order to control and manage the occurrences to the cars. Supported by a backend software, the application receives push notifications when a new emergency is created by the call center of the state. All the occurrences attributed to the car are viewed in a map with the suggested route to arrive at the local of the emergency and also classified by severity. Besides that, when the ambulance arrives at the designated local, the doctors use the application to register pre-attendance information regarding the victim. All these information are synced automatically with the cloud and available to the state's control center as soon as an internet connection is available in the device.

Android Native Development, ORMLite/SQLite, Sync Adapters, AndroidAnnotations (Networking/Multithreading/View Injector), Roboguice (Dependency Injection), Maps, Google Cloud Messaging, Geolocation
